What We Offer

Safe Passage for People,
Valuables & Sensitive Cargo

A professional escort service goes far beyond simply moving a person or package from one location to another. It involves trained security personnel who assess risks before departure, plan the safest possible routes, implement contingency protocols, and provide active, vigilant protection throughout every mile of the journey.

Shellback Marines has refined its escort service operations to address the unique security challenges of the Siliguri region and Northeast India. As a gateway to the Seven Sister States, Siliguri sits at a strategically critical junction — and our deep knowledge of local terrain, traffic patterns, and regional security dynamics makes our escort service particularly valuable to clients operating across this corridor.

Whether you need to move a business executive safely through a sensitive route, transport cash between bank branches, or protect a consignment of high-value industrial equipment, Shellback Marines provides an escort service that is meticulously planned, properly resourced, and executed with the precision of a military operation.

Advance Route Planning

Every escort assignment begins with detailed route analysis, threat point identification, and contingency planning — ensuring the team is prepared for any scenario before the journey begins.

Licensed & Verified Personnel

All escort personnel are background-verified, licensed, and trained in defensive driving, threat recognition, emergency response, and professional conduct — operating under PSA regulations.

Real-Time Communication

State-of-the-art communication systems maintain constant contact between escort teams, our command centre, and clients — enabling rapid response to any situation arising during transit.

In Depth

Why Professional Escort Service
Demands Veteran Expertise

A credible escort service is defined by the quality of its planning and the readiness of its personnel — not simply by the number of vehicles in a convoy. Shellback Marines, founded and led by a retired Indian Navy marine, brings genuine military-grade analytical discipline to every escort engagement. That background matters: our team has been trained to anticipate threats, adapt to changing circumstances, and execute under pressure — skills that translate directly to effective, reliable escort operations.

Technology Behind the Escort Service

Modern escort service operations integrate technology alongside human capability. Shellback Marines equips all escort teams with GPS-tracked vehicles, encrypted real-time communication systems, and direct links to our central command facility. This ensures that route deviations, emerging threats, or unexpected incidents are identified immediately and handled efficiently — giving clients full transparency and peace of mind throughout the transit.

Our vehicle fleet is maintained to the highest standards and configured for specific mission requirements: discreet sedans for low-profile VIP movements, hardened vehicles for high-risk cargo, and armoured options for cash-in-transit operations requiring maximum physical protection.

Regional Expertise in the Northeast India Corridor

Operating from Siliguri, West Bengal, gives Shellback Marines an unmatched advantage in providing escort service across Northeast India. Our teams understand the geographical nuances, seasonal road conditions, jurisdictional boundaries, and political dynamics that can impact security operations in this unique corridor. This local intelligence — combined with established relationships with regional law enforcement — makes our escort service faster, safer, and more reliable than providers without dedicated regional knowledge.

Whether a client needs an escort service from Siliguri to Guwahati, across the Dooars, or into the hill districts of Darjeeling and Kalimpong, Shellback Marines has the personnel, vehicles, and local expertise to execute the mission safely and on schedule.

Customised for Every Client

No two escort service requirements are identical. Shellback Marines works closely with each client to understand their specific operation, assess their risk profile, and design a security solution that delivers maximum protection without disrupting normal business activities. From single-vehicle personal protection to multi-vehicle commercial cargo convoys, we scale every deployment precisely to the requirement — ensuring both effectiveness and operational efficiency.
