Case Study

Shellback Marines — Risk Overhaul for an Industrial Group, North Bengal

A diversified industrial group with three factory sites in North Bengal had grown rapidly but had no formal security risk framework. Following a series of internal theft incidents and the departure of a senior employee with access to sensitive business information, the board commissioned Shellback Marines to conduct a comprehensive risk review across all sites and operations.

We delivered a full threat and vulnerability assessment, identified 14 critical risk areas, and produced a prioritised remediation roadmap. We also implemented immediate measures: deploying additional trained guards, installing access control at three sensitive zones, and delivering security awareness training to all management staff. Six months later, internal theft incidents had reduced to zero and the company's leadership team reported significantly increased confidence in their security posture.
