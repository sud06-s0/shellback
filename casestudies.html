Physical Guarding · Manufacturing

Manufacturing Plant Security, Siliguri Industrial Zone

The Challenge: A large textile manufacturing unit faced repeated after-hours theft of raw materials and equipment from its production yard and perimeter. Previous security arrangements were informal and lacked consistency.

Our Solution: A full security survey revealed three critical perimeter gaps. Shellback Marines deployed static guards at the main gate and two vulnerable points, introduced a randomised mobile patrol cycle, and implemented a full written incident reporting regime.

The Result: All theft incidents ceased within six weeks. Management reported the guard team had become an integral part of site operations, and the contract was made permanent.

Surveillance & Guarding · Commercial

Commercial Complex Integrated Security, Jalpaiguri

The Challenge: A multi-tenanted commercial complex housing retail outlets, office units, and a bank branch had suffered repeated theft and access disputes between tenants. A unified, professional security approach was urgently needed.

Our Solution: Shellback Marines coordinated with building management to implement a unified security plan — deploying on-site guards alongside a structured CCTV monitoring protocol, a single-entry access system, and regular reporting to building management.
