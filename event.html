Services Provided

Event Security
Services Include:

Crowd Management & Control

Structured crowd flow management at entry, exit and key zones to prevent bottlenecks and ensure safety.

Access Control & Ticketing

Managing venue entry points, credential verification, and ticketed admission to prevent unauthorised access.

VIP & Dignitary Protection

Close protection and escort for high-profile guests, speakers, executives, and dignitaries during the event.

Perimeter & Venue Guarding

Static and mobile guard deployment around the venue perimeter to deter intrusion and secure the boundary.

Emergency Response Planning

Pre-planned emergency protocols, evacuation routes, and rapid response teams ready to act at any moment.

Asset & Equipment Protection

Guarding of staging, AV equipment, merchandise, and other high-value assets throughout the event.

Post-Event Security & Dispersal

Managed dispersal, venue sweep, and guard presence during post-event breakdown to prevent theft or disorder.

Case Study

Shellback Marines at a 2,000-Attendee Cultural Festival, Jalpaiguri

A prominent cultural organisation in North Bengal required comprehensive security for their two-day annual festival, attracting nearly 2,000 attendees. Previous years had seen crowd management difficulties at entry gates and isolated incidents during peak hours. They needed a PSA-licensed security partner with proven event experience to plan and lead all security operations.

Shellback Marines conducted a full pre-event site survey and risk assessment, developed a tailored security plan, and deployed a coordinated team across all entry points, crowd zones, VIP areas, and the stage perimeter. Emergency response protocols were briefed to all personnel before Day 1. Both days concluded without a single reportable security incident, and the client specifically commended the professionalism and calm presence of the Shellback Marines team.
