SPECIALIST SECURITY SERVICES

At Shellback Marines, we provide specialist security services tailored to ensure the safety of property and people.

We are committed to providing reliable and professional security solutions across all leading factories, hospitals, banks, and corporate offices — serving both government and private enterprises.

PHYSICAL GUARDING

On-site protection by trained professionals

Trained guards ensure on-site security, deterring unauthorized access and safeguarding premises across factories, commercial complexes, hospitals, and government buildings.

INTELLIGENCE & INVESTIGATION

Insight-driven risk mitigation

Specialized teams gather critical intelligence and conduct thorough investigations, enabling informed decision-making and proactive risk mitigation for our clients.

ELECTRONIC SURVEILLANCE

State-of-the-art remote monitoring

CCTV networks, alarms, and access control systems provide continuous monitoring and rapid response — enhancing overall security and reducing incident risk around the clock.

SECURITY SURVEYS & AUDITS

Security reviews and vulnerability assessment

Comprehensive site assessments identify vulnerabilities and recommend targeted measures to strengthen security protocols — tailored to the unique requirements of each facility.

EVENT SECURITY

Crowd management and access control

Tailored event security solutions including crowd management, perimeter control, and access screening — ensuring a safe environment for attendees, staff, and assets.

CORPORATE RISK MANAGEMENT

Protecting assets, personnel & reputation

A strategic approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating security risks at the corporate level — safeguarding your assets, people, and organizational reputation.

ACCESS CONTROL & CCTV

Authorised entry and continuous recording

We specialise in setting up access control systems and closed-circuit television networks — ensuring only authorised personnel enter designated areas while continuously monitoring activity.

ESCORT GUARDING

Secure transit for individuals and assets

Trained guards accompany clients, sensitive documents, or high-value assets during transit — providing a secure escort and protection against potential threats at every stage.
