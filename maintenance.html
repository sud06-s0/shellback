What We Provide

Protect Your Investment.
Ensure Continuous Protection.

Security technology is only effective when it is working correctly. Cameras with misaligned lenses, alarm systems with faulty sensors, and access control units with degraded hardware are not just ineffective — they create a false sense of security that can be more dangerous than no system at all.

Shellback Marines provides planned preventive maintenance (PPM) programmes, reactive callout services, and system health-check visits that keep your security infrastructure performing to specification. Our technical team carries out maintenance on CCTV systems, intruder alarms, access control, intercoms, and integrated platforms — regardless of the brand or system installed.

Planned Preventive Maintenance (PPM)

Scheduled maintenance visits at agreed intervals — quarterly, bi-annual, or annual — keeping your systems fully operational and extending equipment lifespan.

Fast Reactive Callout Response

When systems fail unexpectedly, our technical team responds promptly to diagnose and restore your security infrastructure — minimising the window of reduced protection.

Multi-Brand System Support

We maintain security systems from all major manufacturers — you don't need to be an existing Shellback Marines installation customer to benefit from our technical maintenance services.

Services Provided

Security Maintenance
Services Include:

CCTV System Maintenance

Camera cleaning, lens alignment, cable inspection, DVR/NVR health checks, recording verification, and software updates for all CCTV networks.

Intruder Alarm Servicing

Full sensor testing, panel diagnostics, battery replacement, communications path testing, and compliance checks for intruder alarm systems.

Access Control Maintenance

Reader testing, door hardware inspection, controller diagnostics, credential database review, and firmware updates for access control systems.

Intercom & Gate System Servicing

Maintenance and repair of video intercoms, audio entry systems, and automated gate and barrier units — including mechanical and electrical components.

System Health Checks & Reports

Comprehensive written health-check reports following every service visit — documenting system status, faults found, actions taken, and any recommended upgrades.

Reactive Fault & Breakdown Response

Priority callout service for unexpected system failures — prompt response to diagnose faults and restore operational status as quickly as possible.

Software & Firmware Updates

Keeping your security system software and firmware current — ensuring compatibility, closing security vulnerabilities, and enabling access to new features.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Fixed-cost annual maintenance contracts covering scheduled PPM visits, parts labour, and priority reactive response — budgetable, reliable, and comprehensive.

Case Study

Shellback Marines AMC — Industrial Plant Security Systems, Jalpaiguri

A large manufacturing plant in Jalpaiguri had invested significantly in a 48-camera CCTV network, intruder alarm system, and card-based access control — but had no formal maintenance contract. Over time, system performance had degraded: eight cameras were not recording correctly, multiple alarm sensors had faults, and two access control readers were non-functional. Management had no visibility of these issues until a theft incident revealed the failures.

Shellback Marines conducted a full system health check, identified all faults, and restored the entire security infrastructure to full working order within three days. We then implemented a quarterly PPM programme under an annual maintenance contract. Eighteen months later, system uptime is consistently above 99%, all faults are identified and resolved proactively before they become security vulnerabilities, and the plant management has full documentary evidence of system status at all times.
