Security Surveys & Audits
Services Include:

Site Security Surveys

Comprehensive on-site surveys assessing physical barriers, access points, lighting, CCTV coverage, and guard post effectiveness.

Security Policy & Procedure Audits

Review and gap analysis of your existing security policies, post instructions, and operational procedures against best practice standards.

Guard Force Effectiveness Audits

Independent assessment of guard deployment, patrol disciplines, post performance, and training compliance — including covert observation where required.

Access Control Audits

Testing and evaluation of existing access control measures — physical, electronic, and procedural — including attempted unauthorised access scenarios.

Technical review of surveillance system coverage, camera placement, image quality, recording configuration, and blind spot mapping.

Perimeter & Physical Security Reviews

Evaluation of fencing, barriers, gates, lighting, and intrusion detection systems protecting the perimeter of your site.

Post-Incident Security Reviews

Independent review following a security incident to identify root causes, assess control failures, and recommend corrective actions.

Scheduled Annual Security Audits

Recurring annual audit programmes ensuring your security posture is continuously reviewed, updated, and aligned with evolving threats.

Case Study

Shellback Marines Security Audit — Private University Campus, West Bengal

A private university in West Bengal with over 4,000 students and staff required an independent security audit ahead of a significant infrastructure expansion. The institution had experienced several incidents of unauthorised access, petty theft, and a serious late-night incident in a car park — but had no independent assessment of their security posture to inform their investment decisions.

Shellback Marines conducted a two-day on-site survey covering all 14 buildings, the external perimeter, four car parks, and all guard posts. We observed guard patrols, tested access control points, assessed CCTV coverage blind spots, and reviewed all security policies and post instructions. The resulting report identified 11 critical findings and 9 recommendations. Following the audit, the university commissioned Shellback Marines to implement six of the priority recommendations, including a CCTV upgrade and revised guard post instructions. No further incidents have been recorded in the 12 months since implementation.
