Effective security is built on intelligence. Without accurate, timely information about the threats facing your organisation, even the best guard deployment remains reactive rather than preventive. Shellback Marines' intelligence and investigative services give you the information advantage — enabling proactive decisions that stop incidents before they occur.

Our specialists bring operational experience from security environments where intelligence is not a luxury but a necessity. We gather, analyse, and present findings in clear, actionable reports that your management team can act on immediately — whether the threat is internal misconduct, external intrusion, or reputational risk.

Case Study

Shellback Marines Investigation — Manufacturing Stock Loss, West Bengal

A large manufacturing facility in Jalpaiguri had experienced significant, unexplained stock losses over several months. Internal CCTV review had failed to identify the source. Management suspected organised internal theft but lacked the evidence to act. Shellback Marines was engaged to conduct a discreet, professional investigation.

Our team deployed covert surveillance, conducted background assessments, and gathered intelligence over a three-week period. The investigation identified a coordinated theft operation involving two members of staff and one contractor. A comprehensive evidence report was delivered to management within 30 days — resulting in successful disciplinary terminations and full recovery of stolen assets. The client subsequently engaged Shellback Marines for ongoing security management.
