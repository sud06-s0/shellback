Our Journey

Service in the Indian Navy

Our founder served with distinction in the Indian Navy as a marine, developing expertise in security operations, threat assessment, and crisis response that would shape Shellback Marines' DNA.

Retirement & a New Mission — 2023

Upon retiring from active service, our founder established Shellback Marines with a clear purpose: to bring military-grade security discipline to India's factories, hospitals, banks, and institutions.

PSA Licence & MSME Registration

Shellback Marines received its Private Security Agency Licence (W.B – 55/WB/PSA/2025) and MSME registration, establishing our legal and professional foundation to operate across West Bengal.

Growing Across 9+ Sectors — Today

Today we protect healthcare institutions, banks, corporates, educational facilities, manufacturing plants, mining operations, government offices, and more — growing steadily on the strength of our reputation.
